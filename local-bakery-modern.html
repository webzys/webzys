About Our Bakery

Sunrise Bakery was founded in 2005 by Jane Doe with a simple mission: to bring delicious, artisanal baked goods to our community. What started as a small shop has grown into a beloved local institution.

We pride ourselves on maintaining traditional baking methods while continuously innovating our recipes. Every item is made from scratch daily using locally sourced ingredients whenever possible.

Our team of passionate bakers wake up early every morning to ensure that our customers can enjoy the freshest breads, pastries, and cakes throughout the day.
